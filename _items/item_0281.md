## The question for students:

and attached to a 2 kg mass as shown. What is the normal force on the 8
kg block? (Use g=10 N/kg)

1. 0 N 
2. 20 N 
3. 40 N 
4. 60 N 
5. 80 N 
6. 100 N 
7. None of above.
